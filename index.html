OUR RICH HISTORY

« RICEKO is not just a restaurant, it’s a culinary landmark steeped in the traditions of Kochani, a city renowned for its exquisite rice. Since opening our doors in 1964, we’ve been more than a dining establishment; we’ve been a celebration of a 60-year-old tradition that continues to thrive. In the heart of Kochani, where the air is fragrant with the scent of homegrown rice, RICEKO stands as a testament to the city’s heritage. Our founders envisioned a place where every meal is a homage to the rice fields that surround us—a vision that has been our guiding star for six decades. As you step into RICEKO, you step into history. Each grain of rice served on your plate comes from the very fields that have defined our city’s landscape and livelihood. Our commitment to tradition is matched only by our dedication to freshness, with rice harvested from our own fields to ensure that every dish carries the essence of Kochani. As we celebrate our 60th anniversary, we reflect on the journey that has brought us here. From the humble beginnings of a small family-owned eatery to becoming a beloved institution. The founders of RICEKO, with their deep-rooted love for Kochani’s traditional rice, embarked on a mission to share this treasure with the world. They believed that the soul of the city could be tasted in every grain, and it was their dream to bring this experience to every table. Our commitment to tradition is unwavering, yet we continually strive to innovate and elevate the culinary experience. We blend time-honored recipes with contemporary techniques, ensuring that while our flavors are rooted in history, they are also vibrant and exciting to the modern palate. As we look to the future, we carry forward the legacy of RICEKO with pride. We invite you to be part of our story, to create new memories, and to savor the taste of tradition that has been our cornerstone for sixty years. Here’s to many more years of sharing the spirit of Kochani with you. »
